**Short answer:** Most Halifax businesses pay between **$800 and $2,500 per month** for ongoing SEO in 2026. Local SEO for a single-location service business typically starts around $800–$1,200/month, while competitive multi-service or multi-location campaigns run $1,500–$2,500+. One-off audits and project work range from $500 to $5,000. What you pay should map directly to how competitive your keywords are and how much work your site needs.

If you're shopping for [Halifax SEO](/) and getting quotes that range from $300 to $5,000 a month, you're not imagining it — the market really is that scattered. Here's what the numbers actually mean and how to spend wisely.

## What Drives the Price of SEO in Halifax

Three factors determine where you land in the range:

1. **Keyword competitiveness.** Ranking for "Halifax dentist" is harder than "denture repair Sackville NS." More competition means more content, more links, and more hours — which costs more.
2. **The starting condition of your site.** A technically broken, thin website needs months of foundational work before rankings move. A clean, established site costs less to grow.
3. **Scope.** Local-only SEO (Google Business Profile plus a handful of pages) is cheaper than a full program covering technical SEO, content, and link building across many service pages.

## Typical Halifax SEO Pricing Models

**Monthly retainer ($800–$2,500/mo).** This is the most common and usually the best value for ongoing growth. You get consistent execution: content, link building, technical maintenance, and reporting every month. SEO compounds, so retainers are built for the long game. **One-off audit ($500–$1,500).** A diagnostic that tells you what's wrong and what to fix. Good if you have an in-house person who can execute the recommendations. (We offer a free version — more on that below.)

**Project-based work ($1,500–$5,000).** A defined deliverable like a technical overhaul, a site migration, or building out 10 location pages. Fixed scope, fixed price.

**Hourly consulting ($100–$200/hr).** Rare for full campaigns, but useful for strategy sessions or training.

## Why "$300/Month SEO" Is a Trap

You'll see Halifax agencies and offshore providers advertising SEO for $200–$400 a month. At that price point, nobody is doing real work. SEO involves skilled labour — competitive research, content writing, manual outreach, technical fixes — and that labour has a floor. At $300/month you're paying for an automated report and a few directory submissions, not rankings.

The damage isn't just wasted money. Cheap providers often build spammy backlinks that can get your site penalized, leaving you worse off than when you started. ## What You Should Actually Get for Your Money

A fair Halifax SEO retainer should include, every month:

- **Technical maintenance** — keeping your site fast, crawlable, and error-free
- **On-page optimization** — titles, headings, internal links, and content updates
- **Content creation** — new pages or posts targeting commercial-intent local keywords
- **Link building** — real outreach to credible Halifax and Nova Scotia sites
- **Google Business Profile management** — posts, photos, review strategy, and Q&A
- **Transparent reporting** — actual ranking data and traffic, not vanity impressions

If a quote doesn't spell these out, ask. A credible provider can tell you exactly where your money goes. ## How to Budget for SEO as a Halifax Business

The smart way to think about SEO cost isn't "what's the cheapest option" — it's "what is one new customer from Google worth to me?" If a new client is worth $2,000 in lifetime value and a $1,200/month campaign brings you three new clients a month, the math is obvious.

Run that calculation before you compare quotes. A higher-priced agency that delivers results is cheaper per lead than a budget provider that delivers nothing.
